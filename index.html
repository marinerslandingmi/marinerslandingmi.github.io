Mariners Landing

This dockside cottage is located in the quaint coastal town of Ludington, on Lake Michigan. It sits adjacent to the home of the SS Badger, the only passenger steamship still in operation in the US; it is the continuation of US 10 and a national historic landmark. The location of this cottage is within walking distance to event venues, museums, parks, marinas, waterfront and all shops, restaurants and breweries in downtown.

Mariners Landing is a two bedroom one bath home with a comfortable kitchen and living room. From mid-May to mid-October, guests are able to view and experience the daily departure and arrival of the SS Badger from various places in and outside of the home. Off season, as the Badger is moored in port, guests can experience picturesque channel and sunset views. Occasionally, guests will witness freighters coming in and out of port and hear the deep sound of a foghorn coming from the Ludington North Breakwater Lighthouse. The dockside cottage offers an appeal to your nautical and maritime interests.
